FEEDING NIGERIA

Food today,Security tomorrow.....!

In a country that wastes billions of pounds of food each year, it's almost shocking that anyone in Nigeria goes hungry. Yet every day, there are millions of children and adults who do not get the meals they need to thrive. We work to get nourishing food – from farmers, manufacturers, and retailers to people in need.
